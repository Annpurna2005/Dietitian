About the Nutritionist

Vasundhara Agrawal

A Bangalore-based diet & active lifestyle expert and an independent food consultant who strives to take the confusion out of healthy eating. Having worked with top hospitals, she believes in tailor-made diet plans for individuals.

A life member of the Indian Dietetic Association, a UGC-NET qualified lecturer, and a corporate trainer, Vasundhara’s mission is to inculcate healthy lifestyle habits among people.

She works with diverse groups and individuals around the World, catering to their special needs, goals or health conditions and helping them lead a healthy life free of worry and illness. With her there is always something for everyone. Therefore, it doesn't matter who you are, where you live, or what you do, she can pave way for a healthier and happier you.
