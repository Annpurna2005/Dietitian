About the Nutritionist

Vasundhara Agrawal

A Bangalore-based diet & active lifestyle expert and an independent food consultant who strives to take the confusion out of healthy eating. She believes in tailor-made diet plans for individuals.

A life member of the Indian Dietetic Association, a UGC-NET qualified lecturer, and a corporate trainer, Vasundhara’s mission is to inculcate healthy lifestyle habits among people.

She works with diverse groups and individuals around the world, catering to their special needs, goals, or health conditions and helping them lead a healthy life free of worry and illness.
